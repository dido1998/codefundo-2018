The Most trusted relief operations service.

We use a combination of Artficial intelligence, image processing and natural language processing to facilitate relief operations in flood-affected areas. We use a smart clustering algorithm so that the NGOs dont have to worry about where to go to save people, We tell them where it is best for them to go.

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE.

We use AI to detect floods from satellite images which are polled from the web in real-time.

SMART.

Worried that your tweets go unnoticed? Don't worry we are here! We poll tweets from twitter and perform sentiment analysis to determine the likelihood of a flood in the area. If a flood is detected from a tweet we will automatically consider that user for rescue.

USER-FRIENDLY.

You don't have to waste time entering your personal details! With a click of a button, we will know that you have to be rescued and you can track the location of your rescuer.

EFFECIENT.

We use extremely effecient clustering algorithms to automatically route NGOs to the people which ensures that no user goes unnoticed and the NGOs are evenly distributed.
